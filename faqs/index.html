What is hair restoration?
Hair restoration is a complex care procedure that allows you to improve the condition of the strands. Usually, restoration is thoughts of after bleaching the hair, after a perm, or coloring.
What is cold hair rehab?
Cold recovery is the treatment of hair with a set of vitamin substances, which works at the cellular level: it fills the hair from the inside with keratin and restores its elasticity and strength.

What is hair lamination?
Hair lamination is a salon procedure for hair recovery. Hair is overcast with a composition that forms an invisible film. As a result, the cuticle is smooth, and the strands look even and smooth.
